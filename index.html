About Kelly Akhigbe

About Kelly Akhigbe I am Kelly Akhigbe, proudly from Edo State, Nigeria — where my passion for education began. Growing up in a family that deeply valued learning, I was taught that knowledge is both a gift and a responsibility to share. My journey started humbly as a home lesson tutor, working with students one-on-one and witnessing the joy that comes from understanding something new. That passion quickly grew, leading me into classroom management and, over the years, into key leadership roles including Examination Officer, Cambridge Coordinator, Director of Academics, and currently, Head of School. Each stage of my career has shaped my perspective, strengthened my expertise, and deepened my belief in the transformative power of education. To me, education is not only about academic success — it’s about shaping character, unlocking potential, and creating opportunities that change lives. Through K and J Education Consult, my mission is clear: to guide, inspire, and empower individuals to thrive and become the best versions of themselves. Every success story I help shape is a powerful reminder that the work we do in education has a lasting impact on people, families, and communities.

we bring a holistic, people-centered approach to every aspect of my work. Whether it's educational consulting, academic mentoring, or counseling, we prioritize empathy, excellence, and integrity.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

To become a trusted global hub for holistic guidance — inspiring academic excellence, nurturing healthy relationships, and empowering individuals to make purposeful life and career choices.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide expert, compassionate, and personalized support through:

  • Innovative educational consultancy and academic mentoring
  • Comprehensive marriage and career counseling
  • Seamless admission guidance for local and international institutions
  • Accessible, high-quality online tutoring

We are committed to unlocking individual potential, promoting lifelong learning, and equipping people with the tools to thrive academically, personally, and professionally.

Why We Do This Work

we do what we do because we believe that every individual deserves the right guidance, support, and opportunity to reach their fullest potential — academically, personally, and professionally.

we passion for education, counseling, and mentorship is rooted in a deep desire to see people grow, overcome challenges, and make informed decisions that shape their future positively. Whether we are guiding a student toward the right academic path, helping a couple navigate their relationship, or supporting someone through a career decision, our goal is always the same: to empower, uplift, and unlock possibilities.

This work gives us a sense of purpose. It allows us to make meaningful impact — one life, one decision, one success story at a time.
